Frequently Asked Questions

What is Amp Rack?

Amp Rack is a Guitar Effects Processor for Android that serves as a LV2 Plugin Host. It supports LV2 SDK v1.1 plugins and provides low latency audio processing for guitars, vocals, and other instruments.

How do I connect my guitar?

You can connect your guitar using a simple 3.5mm adapter (TRS to 1/4"), your phone's built-in mic, or a USB audio interface. Learn more

Can I record my playing?

Yes! Amp Rack supports recording to WAV, MP3, and Opus formats. You can also record backing tracks and play them on loop for practice.

Does it work with USB interfaces?

Absolutely! Amp Rack supports USB audio interfaces. When you plug in a USB device, close and reopen the app to automatically detect it, or manually select it in Settings ‚Üí Audio Devices.

What are presets?

Presets save your entire effect chain configuration including plugin sequence and parameters. You can create unlimited presets and share them publicly with the community.

Is the app really free?

Yes! Amp Rack is completely free and open source. You can download it from the Play Store or build it yourself from the GitHub repository.
